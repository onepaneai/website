Here's the remediation plan for implementing disaster recovery for the Payment API:

Payment API: Disaster Recovery Implementation

1
Deploy Secondary App Service

Create a secondary App Service instance in the East US 2 region to complement your primary instance in West US.

Estimated time: 1-2 hours
2
Configure Traffic Manager

Set up an Azure Traffic Manager profile with priority routing to direct traffic to the secondary region during failover events.

Estimated time: 2-3 hours
3
Implement Automated Deployment

Update your CI/CD pipeline to deploy to both primary and secondary regions simultaneously.

Estimated time: 3-4 hours
4
Test Failover Procedures

Develop and test failover procedures to ensure smooth transition during actual outages.

Estimated time: 2-3 hours
Total Estimated Effort: 8-12 hours
